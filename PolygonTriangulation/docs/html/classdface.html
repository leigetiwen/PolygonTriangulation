vector< pair< dvertex *, int > > dface::sortedVertices ( )

Splits the polygon into left and right parts. Sorts the vertices according to their y-coordinates. The integer in the pair represents whether the vertex is the highest(0), lies in the left part(-1) or the right part(1).
